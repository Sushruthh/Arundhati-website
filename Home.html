Robotic Surgery

Robotic Surgery is a branch of medical science that uses a robotic arm controlled with a computer by a surgeon. Small tools attached to the robotic arm are then used to perform the surgery by copying the hand movements of the surgeon as he controls the robotic arm. With this method being more convenient for the surgeon due to a perfect view of the area under surgery,
Arundathi Hospitals. Robotic Surgery Department is equipped with a skilled team of the best Surgeons. Arundathi Hospitals is your one-stop destination to book the finest Robot-assisted Surgeons in your town.
